How it works

Watch one call go from ring to booked job.

No menus, no hold music. The agent answers, understands the problem, qualifies the job, books it, and drops it into your CRM. Here is a real one.

  1. 01

    Answers in two rings

  2. 02

    Understands the problem

  3. 03

    Qualifies the job

Sample call

AC not cooling

0:51
Thanks for calling Northline Heating and Air, this is the booking line. How can I help?
My AC stopped cooling last night and the house is getting warm.
Sorry to hear that. Is the unit running but blowing warm air, or not turning on at all?
It runs, but it's blowing warm air. It's a Carrier, maybe eight years old.
Got it. I have a technician tomorrow between 8 and 10 in the morning, or today at 4 PM. Which works better?
Tomorrow morning is good.
Booked for tomorrow, 8 to 10 AM. I'll text your confirmation and the technician's name now. Anything else?
Job booked, synced to ServiceTitan Wed 8:00 AM
