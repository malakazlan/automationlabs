## Gaps EVERY competitor leaves open (our differentiation)

1. **No clean, visual, step-by-step call-flow.** Every single one scatters the flow across feature tiles. A clear answer -> qualify -> book -> sync -> follow-up flow, paired with a real transcript, beats all of them. This is exactly what the user wants.
2. **No ROI calculator** (except Johnni, ServiceAgent). We already planned one.
3. **No human-vs-AI vs answering-service comparison table.** Almost none. High-intent, easy win.
4. **Weak or no security/compliance** (call recording consent, encryption, data handling). We flagged this in the spec.
5. **Almost no real photography.** Most use robot mascots, illustration, or text-only. We have a real HVAC photo set. Major advantage.

## Curated additions for our build (the "best parts")

For Home + HVAC (and a dedicated /how-it-works later):
- A. **Live call walkthrough** - real conversation transcript (answer -> qualify -> book -> confirm) + the audio/waveform player. Centerpiece.
- B. **The whole front office, automated** - expand capabilities into the full loop: answer, qualify, book, dispatch/route, follow-up & reminders, review requests, summaries to your team.
- C. **How the agent knows your business** - trained on your services/pricing/service area/FAQs, natural voice, live calendar, emergency escalation rules, encrypted + consent.
- D. **How we get you live** - 3-step onboarding: discovery & call-flow mapping -> we build & train your agent -> go live in 48h + ongoing tuning.
- E. **Results band** - illustrative-labeled outcome metrics.
- F. **Comparison table** - AI agent vs voicemail vs human receptionist vs answering service (cost, hours, speed, booking, scale).
- G. **Integrations grid** - upgrade the wordmark strip to a real grid + "and more".
- H. **Testimonials** - a few illustrative quotes + logo wall.
